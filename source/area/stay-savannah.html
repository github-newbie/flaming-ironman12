Stay Savannah

The Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort & Spa

Enjoy the Savannah Speed Classic, on the grounds of the Westin Savannah Harbor. Overlooking the Savannah River, the resort offers tranquil views and the signature Heavenly Bed, and is just a brief ferry ride to downtown Savannah, to explore historic restaurants, shops and museums. On-site PGA Golf Course and a Conde Nast Traveler “Top Resort Spa” round out your four-star resort experience.

1 Resort Drive, Savannah, GA 31421
