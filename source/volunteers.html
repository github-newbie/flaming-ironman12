Volunteers

Planning for the 13th Annual Motoring Festival, October 24 - November 2, 2014, is currently underway and the Festival is looking for the best volunteers to represent both the event and the Hilton Head Island/Savannah area.

Two Venues - Two Opportunities to help!

This year we will again host events at two different venues over two different weekends:
The Savannah Speed Classic will be held Friday, October 24 - Sunday, October 26 on the Grand Prize of America Road Course on Savannah's Hutchinson Island at The Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort & Spa.

The remainder of the Motoring Festival's events will take place at the Port Royal Golf Club on Hilton Head Island, on Saturday, November 1 and Sunday, November 2.

What is required of Motoring Festival Volunteers

  • Work at least 1 full day (average 8 hours) shift (no 1/2 day shifts available).  However, we encourage you to sign up for as many days as you are available.
  • Provide an email address and phone number (not to be sold to outside vendors) to Motoring Festival management.
    (NOTE: All future correspondence regarding orientation sessions, shirt pick up, etc. will be done via email.)
  • Attend at least one of the one-hour general orientation sessions. ORIENTATION IS REQUIRED and an orientation schedule will be posted (and emailed) at a later date.
  • Report to work no less than 30 minutes prior to the start of your shift.
  • Provide an "In Case of Emergency" contact to Motoring Festival management at time of application.

What are the benefits of being a Motoring Festival Volunteer

  • Official Motoring Fesitval & Concours d'Elegance volunteer shirt.
  • Complimentary admission to the Savannah Speed Classic (October 24 - 26) and the Motoring Festival at the Port Royal Golf Club (November 1 & 2) on days that you are not working.
    (NOTE: Must be present volunteer badge at entry to receive complimentary admission.)
  • Continental breakfast (coffee & danish) and lunch vouchers for volunteers working at the Hilton Head Island venue (Port Royal Golf Club). 
  • Lunch vouchers for volunteers working at the Hutchinson Island venue.
  • Wine and cheese reception following the event for volunteers working at the Hilton Head Island venue (Port Royal Golf Club).
