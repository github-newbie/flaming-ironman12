November 3, 2013

‘Best of Show’ Named at 2013 Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance

Media Contact:
Lindsey Harrell
(843) 785-7469
lharrell@hhiconcours.com

HILTON HEAD ISLAND, S.C. (Nov. 3, 2013) – The coveted ‘Best of Show’ and ‘People’s Choice’ honors were awarded today at the prestigious Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance, held at the Port Royal Golf Club on Hilton Head Island, S.C.

The top honor at Sunday’s Concours d’Elegance, ‘Best of Show,’ was awarded to a 1929 Stutz Supercharged Coupe owned by Richard and Irina Mitchell of Montgomery, Texas. This car is the only example of its kind still in existence.

Attendees selected a Police-equipped 1933 MG L1 as the ‘People’s Choice’ winner. The car is owned by Bill and Sarah Richey of Bowling Green, Ky.

Most Outstanding Car – Pre-1948 went to Bud Frazier’s 1934 Chrysler Airflow CY and Most Outstanding Car – Post-1948 was given to a 1964 Shelby Cobra owned by Ron Finger.

Paul Doerring, the Festival’s Chairman Emeritus, said, “This year’s event filled up in record time. With the notoriety of the event growing year after year and our new venue, the Port Royal Golf Club, we had a higher quality show field. This year’s top honors are a tribute to the growth of the event. We are delighted to add them to the Hilton Head Island history book.”

Adding to the sentiment, Carolyn Vanagel, the Festival’s President, said, “This has been a landmark year for our festival. The event continues to grow each and every year, and 2013 was no exception. The crowds were enthusiastic and enjoyed the wide variety of offerings – presentations, guided walking tours and parades of rare cars. We could not be happier with this year’s turnout.”

More than 500 automobiles made their way to the Hilton Head Island, S.C. Lowcountry this weekend to participate in the 12th Annual Festival. Famed endurance race car driver Hurley Haywood served as the Honorary Chairman and participated at events in both Savannah and Hilton Head Island. Renowned collectors Sam Mann of Englewood, N.J. and Jim Schmidt of Ocala, Fla. were recognized as the 2013 Pinnacle Award Recipient and the Honored Collector Award Recipient respectively.

Motoring Festival Partners for the 2013 Festival included BMW Group NA, Porsche Cars North America, Infiniti, Jaguar Land Rover, Beaufort Memorial Hospital, Hargray, the Hilton Head Island/Bluffton Chamber of Commerce, Visit Savannah and L&L Broadcasting. For more information and a full list of winners, please visit www.HHIMotoringFestival.com.
