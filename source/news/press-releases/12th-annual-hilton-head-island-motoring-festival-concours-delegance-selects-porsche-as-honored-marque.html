12th Annual Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance Selects Porsche as Honored Marque

Media Contact:
Lindsey Harrell
(843) 785-7469
lharrell@hhiconcours.com

Iconic design, performance and racing heritage of Porsche to be showcased throughout 2013 Motoring Festival

HILTON HEAD ISLAND, SC - The Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance, one of the nation’s largest and fastest-growing automotive and motorsports enthusiast event weeks, has selected Porsche as the Honored Marque for the 2013 Festival. Over 62 years of Porsche history, including the cars and personalities that created one of the world’s most iconic brands, will be celebrated throughout the Festival, held October 25 – November 3. 

Both Motoring Festival venues will honor the contributions Porsche has made to the automotive industry: Hutchinson Island’s The Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort & Spa (Savannah Speed Classic) and Hilton Head Island’s Port Royal Golf Club (Concours d’Elegance, Motoring Midway and Car Club Jamboree). 

“With the focus on Porsche motorsport at the Savannah Speed Classic and Porsche classic cars highlighted at the Concours d’Elegance, we offer unique back-to-back opportunities for Porsche enthusiasts, owners and collectors to immerse themselves in the heritage of their favorite brand,” said Carolyn Vanagel, the Festival’s President.  The Motoring Festival and its signature events is one of only very few national automotive gatherings to combine racing, collecting, enthusiast and lifestyle activities within one action-packed week. 

The iconic design, authentic sports car soul and legendary performance of Porsche are all reasons the German automaker has produced some of the world’s most collectible and coveted cars.  As the 2013 Honored Marque, Festival guests will be able to enjoy the complete story board of Porsche evolution, from its earliest roots as a sports car manufacturer to the 50th anniversary of the Porsche 911 this year. 

“The Hilton Head Island Concours is a great way for Porsche to continue the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the 911, and we look forward this memorable occasion,” said Michael Bartsch, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, Porsche Cars North America. Bartsch will also serve as a Guest Judge.

Motoring Festival to Feature Two Judged Classes for Porsche  

The Hilton Head Island-based Concours d’Elegance (Sunday, November 3) will include two judged classes specific to Porsche, including pre-911s and post 356s (through 1973).  In addition, one Concours class of racers will be judged at the Savannah Speed Classic the weekend prior (October 25 - 27) at Hutchinson Island’s “Grand Prize of America” Race Track.  This class will feature a number of racing marques, including Porsche, and the class winners will be recognized at the Concours the following weekend.  The Car Club Jamboree (Saturday, November 2) and the Motoring Midway (November 2 - 3) will also feature un-judged exhibits and car clubs representing Porsche.

Other anniversaries to be celebrated at this year’s Motoring Festival include the 60th anniversary of Corvette and the 110th anniversary of Harley Davidson.

Tickets for the 2013 Motoring Festival will be available for sale online (www.HHIMotoringFestival.com) starting spring 2013, just in time for Father’s Day. 

For more information, visit the official Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance website at www.HHIMotoringFestival.com. 

ABOUT THE HILTON HEAD ISLAND MOTORING FESTIVAL & CONCOURS D’ELEGANCE:

The Hilton Head Island Motoring Festival & Concours d’Elegance will celebrate its twelfth year in the fall of 2013. The Festival kicks off with the Savannah Speed Classic, October 25 – 27, on the Grand Prize of America road course situated on the property of The Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort & Spa.  The festivities will continue on Hilton Head Island, SC, November 2 – 3, at Port Royal Golf Club where the Concours d’Elegance (November 3) will take center stage.  For more information, visit www.HHIMotoringFestival.com online.
